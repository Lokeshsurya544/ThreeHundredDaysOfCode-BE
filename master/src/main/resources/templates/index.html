The mission behind the community.
  1. We believe that learning in isolation can be challenging and demotivating for many people.
  2. We believe that most of us have that innate curiosity, and we fail to realize it due to a lack of good mentors.
  3. The 300 Days Of Code Community is a step toward building a better world - by sharing knowledge across the community.

How can you contribute to the community?
As a part of our community, you can help us in the following:
  1. Share awareness about the community on LinkedIn, Twitter, and Email.
  2. Contribute to the Daily Code Newsletter and help us grow to 100,000 Daily Subscribers. Each blog entry contribution counts.
  3. Invite friends and people you know onto our Discord server.
  4. Give us a shoutout on Twitter, LinkedIn, Substack, and Github.
What are the prizes that you can compete for?
Here are the prizes that you can compete for when solving problems with the Three Hundred Days Of Code community.
  1. Weekly Coding Streak Challenge: Every month, we will give out $50 to the person who submits a solution on the maximum number of days. You need a minimum of 5 unique days to qualify for the prize.
  2. The Coding God: The first person to complete a 300-day-long streak will get a brand new iPhone. The solution should be your own and not copied from any other place.
